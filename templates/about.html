About Us

Our Mission:

We believe that clean streets are a basic right, not a luxury. Our mission is to create garbage-free neighborhoods by enabling citizens to report waste in public areas and holding municipal services accountable for timely clean-up.

What We Do:

This platform allows citizens to easily upload photos of garbage-filled streets, which are then reviewed by our AI system. If garbage is detected, a cleaning request is automatically forwarded to the local municipality. After cleanup, a follow-up image is uploaded and verified by AI to ensure the job was completed.

Why It Matters:

Unclean streets aren’t just an eyesore — they contribute to health hazards, pollution, and community neglect. With your help, we’re using technology to make a real difference, one street at a time.

How It Works:

  • Citizens report by uploading a photo with location.
  • AI confirms presence of garbage → ticket is created.
  • Municipality is notified and cleans the area.
  • Cleanup photo is uploaded and verified.
  • Ticket is closed and visible on the public dashboard.

Whether you're a concerned citizen or a municipal partner, your participation brings us closer to cleaner, healthier communities.
