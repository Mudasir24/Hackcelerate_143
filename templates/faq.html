Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I report garbage on my street?
Log in to our website, click a photo of the overflowing garbage bin, allow location access (or manually enter the address), and submit the form.
2. What happens after I submit a report?
Your image is processed by our AI, which verifies if the garbage is full, overflowing, or already clean. If validated, the report is sent to the concerned officer for further action.
3. How do I know if my report is being acted on?
You can check the status of your report in your personal dashboard. The officer uploads an “after cleaning” image once the task is done.
4. Can I report anonymously?
No. You must create an account and log in to submit a report. This ensures accountability and tracking of reports.
5. What if the garbage is not cleaned properly?
If an area still appears unclean after an officer’s update, you can report it again. The system currently treats each report as a separate entry.
6. Who is responsible for the cleaning?
Officers (garbage collection personnel) are assigned specific areas. Once a report from their zone is validated, it becomes visible on their dashboard for action.
7. Is this available in all cities?
Currently, the system is operational in Hyderabad only. Expansion to other cities is planned in the future.
8. Is there any reward system for regular users?
While there’s no reward system yet, our dashboard highlights the Top 10 Contributors — users who’ve submitted the most verified reports.
